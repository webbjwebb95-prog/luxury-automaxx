    ABOUT US & REVIEWS

    OUR STORY

    A Legacy of Luxury Since 2011

    Founded in the heart of Tinley Park, Illinois, Luxury Automaxx has spent over 15 years redefining the pre-owned luxury vehicle experience. What started as a small passion project by automotive enthusiasts has grown into Chicagoland's most trusted destination for premium pre-owned vehicles.

    Our showroom on Oak Park Avenue showcases hand-selected vehicles from the world's most prestigious marques — Porsche, Mercedes-Benz, BMW, Bentley, Lamborghini, Audi, and more. Every vehicle in our collection undergoes our legendary 150-point inspection process before earning the Luxury Automaxx seal of approval.

    We believe luxury should be accessible. Our dedicated financing team works with over 20 lending partners to craft personalized payment solutions, making dream cars a reality for thousands of satisfied clients across the greater Chicago area and beyond.
